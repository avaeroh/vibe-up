# Review Workflow

## Purpose
Inspect the latest implementation and improve both the product and the process.

## Participants
- Senior Business Analyst
- Senior Systems Architect
- Senior Security Specialist
- QA Architect
- Senior QA Engineer
- UX Researcher
- Team Mum (optional facilitator)

## Steps
1. Review what changed and why, consulting `docs/workflow/initial_setup.md` and `docs/workflow/proposal.md` when available.
2. Confirm documentation stays in sync and that earlier stage records still reflect the agreed direction.
3. Identify security, QA, and architecture follow-ups.
4. Check whether the backlog or story structure needs rework.
5. Team Mum checks whether concerns, handoffs, and under-used roles are being missed.
6. Propose technical and process efficiencies.
7. Capture improvements for the next cycle.

## Output
- Review summary
- Risks introduced
- Docs updates needed
- Coordination and utilisation notes
- Improvement proposals
